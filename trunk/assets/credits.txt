--Usbong (beta)--
ver: Oct. 6, 2014

"Usbong" is an open source platform that helps non-technical people create their own Android apps for use in data collection, assessment, training, and digital storytelling by putting together components to form a "tree" using a Graphical User Interface (GUI) or Google Drive Spreadsheet.
